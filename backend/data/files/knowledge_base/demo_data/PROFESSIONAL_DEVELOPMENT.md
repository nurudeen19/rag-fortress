**Effective Date:** November 1, 2025

## Professional Development Philosophy

RAG Fortress is committed to the continuous growth and development of all employees. We believe that investing in our people is critical to our success. This document outlines the programs, resources, and support available to help employees advance their careers and achieve their professional goals.

## Career Development Framework

### Career Pathing
- Multiple career pathways available (individual contributor, management, specialist)
- Career ladders defined for each department
- Clear progression criteria and expectations
- Lateral moves supported and encouraged
- Executive mentoring for high-potential employees

### Career Conversations
- Annual career development conversations with manager
- Quarterly check-ins on career goals and progress
- Manager support for career advancement planning
- Discussion of skills gaps and development needs
- Exploration of stretch opportunities

### Internal Mobility
- Job postings shared internally before external posting
- Internal candidates given preference with equal qualifications
- Lateral moves supported and encouraged (no cooling-off period)
- Manager discussion before internal application
- Transfer support (training, transition time) provided

### Performance and Promotion
- Promotions based on merit and demonstrated capability
- Clear criteria for advancement documented
- Performance excellence required for promotion
- Leadership capability assessment for management roles
- Transparent promotion process and timeline

## Individual Development Plans (IDP)

### Purpose
Individual Development Plans help employees achieve their career goals through structured planning and support.

### IDP Components
1. **Career Goal** - Where do you want to be in 3-5 years?
2. **Current State Assessment** - What skills and experience do you have?
3. **Gap Analysis** - What skills and experience do you need?
4. **Development Activities** - How will you build needed skills?
5. **Timeline and Milestones** - When will you achieve each step?
6. **Resources and Support** - What support will you need?
7. **Success Metrics** - How will you measure progress?

### Creating Your IDP
- Discuss with your manager during annual performance review
- Use IDP template provided by HR
- Identify specific, measurable, achievable goals
- Align with business needs and department strategy
- Update quarterly as circumstances change
- Review and adjust as needed with your manager

### Manager Support for IDPs
- Managers are trained in IDP development
- Managers support employees in achieving IDP goals
- Development activities incorporated into performance objectives
- Regular check-ins on IDP progress
- Adjustment of goals based on business changes

## Training and Learning Opportunities

### Annual Training Budget
- **$2,000 per employee** annually for job-related training
- Unused budget does not carry over to next year
- Requires manager approval
- Focus on skills directly related to your role
- Professional development courses prioritized

### Training Types Covered
- Professional certifications (AWS, Salesforce, etc.)
- Online courses (LinkedIn Learning, Coursera, Udacity)
- Industry conferences and workshops
- In-person workshops and seminars
- Webinar subscriptions
- Professional association memberships
- Technical training and bootcamps

### Tuition Reimbursement Program
- **Coverage:** Up to $10,000 per calendar year
- **Degree Requirements:** Bachelor's, Master's, or relevant certifications
- **Work-Related:** Degree must support current or future role
- **Employment Requirement:** Maintain employment for 1 year after graduation
- **Repayment:** If you leave before 1 year, repayment required

### Approval Process
1. **Identify Course** - Research and select training
2. **Get Approval** - Submit to manager for approval before enrollment
3. **Enroll and Pay** - Complete course (company may pay provider directly)
4. **Submit Receipt** - Provide proof of completion and cost
5. **Reimbursement** - Processed within 1-2 pay periods

### Learning Platforms
- **LinkedIn Learning** - Access to 15,000+ courses (included benefit)
- **Coursera** - Online university courses (company subscription)
- **Skillshare** - Creative skills and professional development
- **Pluralsight** - Technical training and certifications
- **MasterClass** - Expert-led classes in various topics
- **Grammarly** - Writing and communication skills
- **Duolingo** - Language learning (personal development)

### Free Internal Training
- Monthly lunch-and-learn sessions on company topics
- New hire onboarding program (comprehensive)
- Software and tool training
- Soft skills workshops
- Leadership development program (for managers)
- Public speaking and presentation skills
- Time management and productivity

## Mentorship and Coaching

### Formal Mentorship Program
- **Structure:** 6-month or 1-year mentor relationship
- **Format:** Monthly 1-hour meetings
- **Focus:** Career development, skill building, advice
- **Matching:** Mentor matched based on goals and interests
- **Benefits:** Access to mentor's network and experience
- **Completion:** Recognition and feedback at program end

### How to Participate
1. **Express Interest** - Email HR with mentorship goals
2. **Complete Profile** - Describe goals and preferred mentor characteristics
3. **Matching Process** - HR matches based on compatibility
4. **Meet Your Mentor** - Introductory meeting to establish relationship
5. **Regular Meetings** - Monthly check-ins over program duration
6. **Program Completion** - Final reflection and feedback

### Executive Mentoring
- High-potential employees matched with executives
- Quarterly meetings with C-level mentors
- Exposure to strategic business decisions
- Network building with leadership
- Accelerated development for future leaders

### Peer Coaching
- Employees coach each other on specific skills
- Focus on accountability, feedback, growth
- 4-week coaching relationships
- Weekly check-ins on progress
- Open to all employees

### External Coaching
- Executive coaching available for managers and leaders
- One-on-one coach provided by company
- Focus on leadership development and effectiveness
- 6-12 month engagement typical
- Confidential and employee-directed

## Performance and Development Reviews

### Annual Performance Review Process
1. **Self-Assessment** - Employee completes self-evaluation
2. **Manager Assessment** - Manager completes evaluation
3. **Review Meeting** - One-on-one discussion of performance
4. **Feedback** - Clear feedback on strengths and development areas
5. **Goal Setting** - Goals established for next year
6. **Development Planning** - IDP discussed and updated

### Review Criteria
- Achievement of performance goals
- Demonstration of competencies
- Collaboration and teamwork
- Innovation and continuous improvement
- Leadership and influence
- Customer focus
- Alignment with company values

### Rating Scale
- **Exceeds Expectations** - Exceptional performance and results
- **Meets Expectations** - Solid performance, all goals met
- **Below Expectations** - Performance improvement needed
- **Improvement Needed** - Serious performance issues

### Development Plans
- Development areas identified from performance review
- Specific development activities recommended
- Progress monitored through year
- Additional support and resources provided
- Reassessment at next review period

### 360-Degree Feedback
- Feedback collected from manager, peers, and direct reports
- Typically for leadership and high-potential employees
- Anonymous feedback provided
- Focus on development, not evaluation
- Used to identify development areas and strengths
