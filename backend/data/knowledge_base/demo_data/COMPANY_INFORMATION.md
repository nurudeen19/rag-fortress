## Company Overview

### Our Company
**RAG Fortress Analytics** is a leading provider of enterprise information retrieval and knowledge management solutions. We empower organizations to unlock the value of their data through advanced retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) technology and intelligent document management systems.

**Founded:** 2020  
**Headquarters:** San Francisco, California  
**Employees:** 150+  
**Service Areas:** North America, Europe, Asia-Pacific

### Mission Statement
To revolutionize how organizations access, understand, and leverage their knowledge assets through cutting-edge retrieval-augmented generation technology and intelligent information management systems.

### Vision Statement
To be the globally recognized leader in enterprise knowledge management, enabling organizations of all sizes to make data-driven decisions with unprecedented speed and accuracy.
